# Best Practices for Stat Expansion & Governance

- Always simulate and govern every stat/market you see on the board.
- Use historical data and context (pace, role, volatility) for each stat.
- Pass all simulation outputs through the governance gate (ESS, minute stability, blowout risk).
- Only surface picks that pass the ESS threshold for that stat/market.
- Track and audit failures by stat type (FAS) to tune your system.
- Update reporting and cheat sheets to include all eligible markets.
- Document every new stat integration and update your governance guide.
- Use the provided templates and checklist for every new stat type.
