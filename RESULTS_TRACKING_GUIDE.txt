📊 RESULTS TRACKING SYSTEM - QUICK GUIDE

You have 3 tools to track picks performance:

1️⃣ CHECK RESULTS (View historical performance)
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe check_results.py
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe check_results.py 2026-01-02
   
   Shows:
   - Overall hit rate
   - Performance by tier (SLAM, STRONG, LEAN, BELOW)  
   - Performance by direction (OVER vs UNDER)
   - Top performing picks
   - Biggest misses with failure causes

2️⃣ UPDATE RESULTS (Enter actual game outcomes)
   
   Show pending picks:
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe update_results.py show 2026-01-02
   
   Update single pick:
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe update_results.py update pick_2026010201_og_anunoby_points 22.0
   
   Bulk update from CSV:
   .venv\Scripts\python.exe update_results.py bulk jan02_results.csv

3️⃣ VIEW RAW DATA
   Open: calibration_history.csv
   
   Contains all picks with:
   - Predictions (probability, tier, line)
   - Results (actual_value, outcome: HIT/MISS)
   - Analysis (failure causes, governance flags)
