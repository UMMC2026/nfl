    Raw P(hit) × Shrinkage Factor = Governed Confidence

    < 10 games:    Raw × 0.65  (aggressive shrinkage)
    10-20 games:   Raw × 0.80  (moderate shrinkage)
    20-30 games:   Raw × 0.90  (light shrinkage)
    30+ games:     Raw × 1.00  (full confidence, up to cap)

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                        PARLAY CORRELATION PENALTY                            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

    ENTRY EV = Π(P_leg) × Correlation_Penalty × Stat_Class_Penalty × Payout

    Correlation_Penalty:  Player/stat correlations (existing logic)
    Stat_Class_Penalty:   NEW governance enforcement

    If 2+ sequence_early legs:  Penalty × 0.85  (-15% EV)
    If 2+ event_binary legs:    Penalty × 0.80  (-20% EV)

    Example:
        OG Anunoby (core) + Giannis (core) + Shead (core)
        → Stat_Class_Penalty = 1.0 (no penalty, all core)
        → Combined prob = 42.2% × 1.0 = 42.2%

        If we added early_3min_pts + another early stat:
        → Stat_Class_Penalty = 0.85  (-15%)
        → Combined prob = 42.2% × 0.85 = 35.9%
