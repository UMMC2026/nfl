## HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS

### Step-by-Step Flow

1. **User inputs slate** (real player names, stats, lines)
2. **Slate automation** creates JSON and triggers pipeline
3. **Pipeline loads** slate and props
4. **For each prop:**
   - ✅ Check injury status (gates)
   - ✅ Hydrate 10 recent games from nflverse
   - ✅ Calculate mu & sigma from real stats
   - ✅ Compute P(hit) using Normal CDF
   - ✅ Apply injury confidence multiplier if needed
5. **Output:** Cheatsheet with qualified picks (P(hit) ≥ 65%)
