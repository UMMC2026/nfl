LAYERED DEFENSE STRATEGY (Defensive Depth)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Layer 1: CONFIDENCE CAPS (prob.py)
  - Prevents any single stat from claiming >75% confidence
  - Example: Player points prop can't exceed 75% even if model says 90%
  
Layer 2: SAMPLE SIZE PENALTIES (calibration.py)
  - Shrinks confidence for stats with <20 games of data
  - Example: New player on team → confidence reduced 10–20%
  
Layer 3: VOLATILITY PENALTIES (calibration.py)
  - Reduces confidence for high-variance stats
  - Example: Three-point attempt props (high variance) → penalty applied
  
Layer 4: CORRELATION PENALTIES (daily_pipeline.py)
  - Reduces combined probability when picks move together
  - Example: Player points + Player PRA are correlated → penalty applied
  
Layer 5: TICKET EXPOSURE LIMITS (ticket_governor.py) ← NEW
  - Prevents overconcentration in single tickets
  - Example: Max 2 alt-stats per parlay (not 3+)
  
Layer 6: SLATE-LEVEL EXPOSURE (Planned Phase C-2)
  - Prevents overconcentration across entire slate
  - Example: Max 10% EV from alt-stats total (across all tickets)
